Why Choose SimpleMoxieSwitcher for Your Moxie Robot?

SimpleMoxieSwitcher is the leading free alternative for controlling your Moxie robot by Embodied Inc. Unlike cloud-based Moxie AI services that require monthly subscriptions, our open Moxie software runs completely on your local computer, giving you total privacy and control over your child's Moxie companion robot.

Complete Moxie Robot Features

Our Moxie control software includes everything you need: AI-powered conversations using OpenAI, Anthropic, or local models; educational games including trivia, spelling, and movie quotes; multilingual support for language learning; interactive storytelling; smart home integration with Alexa and Google Home; comprehensive parental controls with PIN protection; usage analytics and cost tracking; and complete appearance customization for your Moxie robot.

Moxie Robot Privacy & Security

As a local-first Moxie application, all your data stays on your computer. No telemetry, no tracking, no cloud storage of your child's conversations with their Moxie AI companion. Perfect for parents who want the benefits of a Moxie robot without sacrificing privacy or paying recurring subscription fees.

Multi-Platform Moxie Control

SimpleMoxieSwitcher works on macOS 13.0+, Windows 10/11, and iOS 16.0+. The iOS remote app lets you control your Moxie robot from anywhere in your home. All versions include automated Docker and OpenMoxie backend setup, making it easy to get started with your Embodied Moxie robot.
