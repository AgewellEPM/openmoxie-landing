The Problem

Moxie robot owners faced a frustrating limitation: changing appearances and personalities required navigating through the official app, waiting for content updates, and dealing with subscription barriers. Parents wanted more control over their $800+ investment.

The Discovery

Through reverse engineering and network analysis, we discovered Moxie communicates via MQTT and HTTP APIs. The robot's configuration is stored in simple JSON files, making it possible to create direct control interfaces without cloud dependencies.

The Solution

Open Moxie 2.0 was born - a native macOS application providing instant access to all 70+ features, 55 appearance options, and 11 personalities. Built with zero dependencies, 1,723 lines of carefully crafted code, and a plastic toy aesthetic that matches Moxie's playful nature.

The Community

Released under MIT license, Open Moxie 2.0 became the foundation for the OpenMoxie community. Parents, developers, and Moxie enthusiasts worldwide now contribute features, share customizations, and help each other get the most from their robots.

Our Mission

To empower Moxie robot owners with complete control over their hardware investment, free from artificial limitations and subscription barriers. We believe in open source, community collaboration, and the right to customize technology you own.
